Monthly Data on India’s International Trade in Services for the Month of October 2019

The Reserve Bank releases monthly data on India’s international trade in services with a lag of around 45 days.

The value of exports and imports of services during the month of October 2019 are given in the following Table.

Table: International Trade in Services
(US$ Million)
Month Receipts (Exports) Payments (Imports)
July-2019 19,084 12,829
August-2019 18,244 12,006
September-2019 17,536 11,096
October-2019 17,698 10,864
Note: Data are provisional.

Monthly data on services are provisional and would undergo revision when the Balance of Payments (BoP) data are released on a quarterly basis.

Rupambara
Director
