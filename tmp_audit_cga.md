## Gaps
- **2016-07 missing (both series).** /MonthlyReport/Published/7/2016-2017.aspx returns the unpublished-month shell page (no data); alternate URL variants also empty, PIB archive for Aug-2016 release not reachable via search, web.archive.org blocked. The Jul-2017 report's paren shows Apr–Jul 2016 FD was 73.7% of BE, but per the "as printed in that month's report" definition the observation was skipped, not imputed.

## Flags
- **RE replaces BE in late-FY reports (recorded as printed, same convention as existing 2025-01..03 / 2026-01..03 obs).** Denominator switch months (fiscal-deficit denominator, ₹ cr): FY16-17 from 2017-02 (533904→534274); FY17-18 from 2018-01 (546532→594849); FY18-19 from 2019-01 (624276→634398); FY19-20 from 2020-01 (703760→766846); FY20-21 from 2021-01 (796337→1848655, COVID RE — explains FD% dropping 145.5→66.8 between Dec-2020 and Jan-2021); FY21-22 from 2022-01; FY22-23 from 2023-01; FY23-24 from 2024-01. Capex denominators switch in the same months.
- **FY19-20 additionally switched BE vintage mid-year:** Apr–Jun 2019 reports use the Feb-2019 interim-budget BE (FD 703999), Jul-2019 onward the Jul-2019 full-budget BE (703760).
- **>100% of BE readings are genuine, as printed:** 2017-01/02 (105.7/113.4), 2017-11..2018-02 (112.0–120.3), 2018-10..2019-02 (103.9–134.2), 2019-03 (101.7, vs RE), 2019-10..2020-02 (102.4–135.2), 2020-03 (122.0, vs RE), 2020-07..2020-12 (103.1–145.5).
- **FYTD capex declines month-on-month twice, as printed (both re-derive exactly from the reports' own BE×% cells):** 2016-09→2016-10 (1.349→1.250 lakh cr; Non-Plan capital restated 56799→42274 cr) and 2018-02→2018-03 (2.971→2.637 lakh cr; March-2018 accounts netted large adjustments — 2.637 matches FY18 final actual capex ₹2.63 lakh cr).
- **Prior-year paren mismatches (all Jan/Mar):** the 8 fisc_def and 6 capex paren mismatches are later-vintage restatements of the prior year (e.g. Mar-2018 report shows prior-year (100.7%) where the Mar-2017 report printed 100.1%); values recorded are always the ones printed in the month's own report.
- **FY2016-17 capex definition:** reports pre-date the FY18 abolition of Plan/Non-Plan; capex taken as Non-Plan "On Capital Account" + Plan "On Capital Account" (BE sum 247024 cr = FY17 BE capex ₹2.47 lakh cr, confirming the mapping).
