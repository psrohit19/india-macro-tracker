## Gaps / flags

- gnpa: values are the figures as FIRST PRINTED in each half-year's own FSR (matches the seed convention in pipeline/fetchers/rbi_fsr.py). RBI restated three of them by 0.1-0.2 pp in the following FSR: Mar-2016 7.6 -> 7.8, Sep-2016 9.1 -> 9.2, Mar-2018 11.6 -> 11.5. Not applied; noted here.
- ies: two rounds in the consolidated xlsx differ by 0.1 from the figures printed in the original releases (later re-estimation): May-2021 seeded 10.8 (xlsx 10.848; original release printed 10.9) and Nov-2021 seeded 12.7 (xlsx 12.658; original releases printed 12.6). Consolidated xlsx used throughout for internal consistency (it matches every 2022+ seed obs).
- ies/ccs cadence: quarterly rounds through Sep-2016 (Mar/Jun/Sep), then a Nov-2016 round starts the bi-monthly MPC-aligned cycle; 2016-17 transition years have both Nov AND Dec rounds (both real, both seeded). Cadence settles at Jan/Mar/May/Jul/Sep/Nov from 2020 (2019 has a Jul round instead of Jun-cycle). No rounds were skipped during COVID, but Mar-2021..Jan-2022 CCS/IES rounds were conducted telephonically.
- ccs: the six telephonic rounds Mar-2021..Jan-2022 were revised upward ~1-1.8 pts by RBI after the original releases (e.g. Jul-2021 all-time low printed 48.6, revised 49.5; Jan-2022 printed 63.7, revised 64.4). Seeded values are the revised, current-official series from the Nov-2023 consolidated xlsx. Pre-2021 values are identical across release vintages.
- ccs level: 2016-2018 CSI sits near/above 100 and the 2020-21 COVID trough is ~50-64 — a much wider historical range than the 85-98 of the 2023+ seed window; same definitional urban CSI series (verified continuous through the consolidated table).
- obicus_cu: first prints get revised as late responses arrive; the consolidated series is used (it is what the existing seed already matches). Largest such revision found: Q4:2015-16 printed 74.1 in the Jun-2016 release (id 17116), 75.5 in the consolidated table (seeded 75.5). COVID crash Q1:2020-21 = 47.3 as expected.
- ios: RBI re-released the 2018-19..2019-20 rounds as "( Revised )" and the consolidated Table 23 differs from original first prints by up to ~3 pts in 2018-2020 (e.g. BEI for Q2:2018-19 printed 114.1 originally vs 111.7 consolidated; BEI for Q2:2020-21 printed 99.5 vs 101.7 consolidated). Seeded values = consolidated Table 23 (exactly consistent with all existing 2022+ seed obs). BEI dated by round-conduct quarter per the rbi_surveys.py convention.
- Depth beyond 2016 is available in the same consolidated xlsx files (IES to 2009, CCS to 2015-03/2012-09, OBICUS to 2008, IOS to 2000) if ever wanted; deliberately not seeded to stay within task scope.
