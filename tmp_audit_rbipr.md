## Gaps / definition floors
- No gap months in any of the three series — all contiguous monthly runs.
- svc_exports: 2015-10/11/12 come from the trailing-month tables of releases listed in the Jan/Feb-2016 archive months; the release series continues before 2016 but the walk floor was 2016-01 per task. Values are provisional pending quarterly BoP reconciliation (RBI's own note); Commerce/PIB monthly services numbers are ministry estimates and intentionally NOT used.
- walr: fresh-rupee-loans WALR begins Sep-2014 — the start of RBI's published table; nothing earlier exists on this definition. Monthly "Lending and Deposit Rates" releases only exist from Jul-2021; Jul-2021..Jun-2022 releases have no numbers in the body (XLSX annexure only), and pre-Jul-2021 the monthly release was MCLR-only (no WALR) — hence the annexure of the LAST annexure-era release (Jun-2022) supplies 2014-09..2022-05 in one later-vintage print. Outstanding-loans WALR never substituted.
- walr annexure vintage: Jul-2021 vs Jun-2022 annexures differ 1-12bps for the 30 months 2019-01..2021-06 (identical before 2019); the later (Jun-2022) vintage is used throughout.

## Flags
- personal_loans MERGER BASIS: every sectoral release from Jul-2023 data through May-2025 data footnotes ALL highlight growth rates as "Excluding the impact of the merger of a non-bank with a bank" (HDFC/HDFC Bank, footnote 3 attached to the "Highlights" heading). The release body prints NO including-merger personal-loans figure, so the headline excluding-merger print is recorded — this is also the basis of the pre-existing obs (2025-03+ releases carry the same footnote), so the series is basis-continuous, but 2023-07..2024-06 y-o-y understates including-merger growth.
- personal_loans panel/format breaks (values kept as-printed per task rule; later releases' year-ago quotes recomputed on new panels and differ): (i) Jun-2017 "Revised Data for April 2017" reissue (panel 46→41 banks) — 2017-04 recorded as 14.4 from the reissue (original print 14.8); (ii) w.e.f. Jan-2021 "revised format" — 2021 releases quote 2020 year-ago growth ~1-2pp below the 2020 as-printed prints (e.g. 2020-07 printed 11.2, re-quoted 9.0); (iii) Dec-2016 release quotes Dec-2015 as 18.5 vs 16.1 as printed in the Dec-2015 release; (iv) May-2025 release quotes May-2024 as 19.3 vs 17.8 as printed (merger-basis recomputation). Nov-2023 risk-weight change: no wording/basis change in the releases themselves.
- personal_loans: from the Nov-2024 release the sentence dates growth "as on the fortnight ended <last reporting Friday>" — same reporting-Friday convention, no basis change. From Dec-2025 (already-existing obs) the reporting fortnight is calendar month-end (Banking Laws (Amendment) Act 2025), as documented in rbi_pr.py.
- svc_exports: monthly values revise (pro-rata to quarterly BoP) — the backfill takes each month's LAST print within the walked window (through the 2025-06 archive month); existing obs (2024-04 onward) are later vintages still and always win.
- Merge rule applied as specified: existing obs untouched (26/15/15 kept verbatim), only older months added, compact JSON, top-level keys preserved. AUDIT.md, fetcher code and other series untouched.
